About Blood Donation

Blood donation is a simple, safe, and life-saving act. Every two seconds, someone needs blood, and one donation can save up to three lives. Blood cannot be manufactured – it can only come from generous donors like you.

Why Regular Donation Matters

Regular blood donations ensure a stable and sufficient blood supply at all times. It's especially critical during emergencies, surgeries, childbirth, and for patients with chronic diseases like cancer and anemia.

User Awareness

  • Donate blood once every 3 months if you're healthy and aged 18–60.
  • Stay hydrated, eat a healthy meal, and get enough rest before donating.
  • Spread awareness among friends and family to increase the number of donors.
  • Join local donation drives or register as a regular donor with hospitals and NGOs.

Together, we can save more lives!
